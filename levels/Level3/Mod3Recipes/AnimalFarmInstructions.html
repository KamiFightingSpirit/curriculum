Abstract Animals

Follow the instructions below
Warning: make sure that all your classes are in the farm package or you may have unexpected results.

  1. Create an Abstract class "Animal"
  2. In the Animal class:

    • create 1 abstract method called makeNoise
    • create a method of your choosing that is common to all farm animals
  3. Create 4 subclasses of Animal (Pig, Cow, etc.)

  1. Create a Farm class with a main method
  2. In the main method

    • Make an ArrayList of Animals called farm
    • Add 6 Animals to your farm
    • Use a loop to call makeNoise and your custom method for each animal
